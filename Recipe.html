Recipe

Crispy Pasta

Ingredients

  • Take the pasta
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes, crushed
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until it is soft. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and sautÃ© until fragrant.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to combine.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil and grated Parmesan if desired.
  6. Voila!!! Serve it.
